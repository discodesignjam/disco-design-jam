Participants

This page is for participants of a DISCO design jam. Here, you can find various briefing materials relevant to your workshop and its design prompt, including:

  • Ship Cards

  • Crew Persona Cards

  • Crew Persona Highlights

Ship Decks

Ship Type Cards

Below you will find cards of various ship types. The ship’s purpose will provide your crew with, as well as what kind of spaces you will be designing to fit said crew. Find the blueprint drawn by your workshop lead to learn more about it.

General Ship Elements

This document, marked “FOR ALL SHIPS” and living within the Ship Blueprint folder, contains a list of elements/spaces that are required in every ship, regardless of its purpose. You can reference this list to read more about the general spaces your DISCO jam lead has selected for your group to design, alongside spaces from your Ship Type card. (The exact number, and which ones, depends on your workshop group size: your jam facilitator will tell you.)

Crew Persona Deck

This deck is full of different crew member persona cards ranging from Wyverns to Space Otters to Grunks. Find the persona cards drawn by your DISCO leader to learn more about crew members’ personalities, preferences, and access needs, and inform the design of your ship. (Pay attention to which crew members are hired to which roles. Which areas on your ship will their needs be most relevant to?
